The Band Camino members are JefferyJordan (27), Spencer Stewart (29), and Garrison Burgees (27). The band started its musical journey in 2015. 

They released their debut album Elektra in 2021 which features their radio-friendly single “Sorry Mom” their single was praised and lauded by critics and they were dubbed to be rising indie rock stars. 

Jeffery Jordan

Jeffery Jordan is a student at the University of Memphis, he was one of the founders and vocalists of the band. He is a 27-year-old talented artist who uses low tenor in his voice. 

Initially, the band was started by Jordan and Stewart who were friends from their college University of Memphis but the band made their move to Nashville in 2018 and has been releasing music since then. 

In 2021 Jeffery and his band also performed at Jimmy Kimmel live, they performed the song titled “Last Cigarette” which received a wide array of praise for their respective performances. 

Spencer Stewart

Spencer Stewart is another founder of the band. The 29-year-old shares vocal duties with Jordan. He is a countertenor singer who uses his unique vocal rhythm, which makes the band’s music quite unique. 

He shared his songwriting and singing talents into releasing their first EP titled “My Thoughts On You” which consists of songs like Young, 2/14, Free of Charge, The Black and White, and For a While.

Since, their struggle and present success, the band has released multiple albums and singles. They have become more prolific, experienced, and optimistic about their band’s future.

Garrison Burgees

Garrison Burgees is a 27-year-old drummer and bass player since 2017. He joined the band after the original drummer of the band Andrew Isbell left to pursue other avenues.

The trio has garnered quite a success even after their obvious disadvantages. They have become successful while releasing their indie, electro-pop music, which the band has described themselves as “Rock Pop.”

Burgess and his band receive wide popularity and support from the Memphis and Nashville community. They have also performed in multiple tours and music festivals which have significantly increased their hype and the attention of the audience. 

Furthermore, the other past members of the band include their drummer Andrew Isbell who left the band in 2017, he is engaged in songwriting and music production with his girlfriend and other artists. 

Similarly, Graham Rowell was accused of sexual harassment, he left the band in 2020 since then he has washed his hands from music and has remained focused on his civil life.
