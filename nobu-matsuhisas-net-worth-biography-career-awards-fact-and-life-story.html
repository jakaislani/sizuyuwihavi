Nobu Matsuhisa net worth: Nobu Matsuhisa is a Japanese chef and restauranteur who has a net worth of $200 million. Nobu Matsuhisa was born in Saitama, Japan in March 1949. He is known for creating traditional Japanese dishes which are blended with Peruvian ingredients and his signature dish is his black cod in miso. He owns the Matsuhisa Restaurants and Nobu Hospitality with his family as well as the Nobu Restaurants with Robert De Niro among others. Those restaurants include Matsuhisa Aspen, Matsuhisa Vail, Matsuhisa Denver, Matsuhisa Beverly Hills, Nobu Dallas, Nobu Las Vegas, Nobu Lanai, Nobu Los Angeles, Nobu Malibu, Nobu Miami Beach, Nobu New York, Nobu Next Door, Nobu Fifty Seven, Nobu San Diego, and Nobu Honolulu. He also owns Matsuhisa locations in Greece and Germany and Nobu locations in The Bahamas, Dubai, Qatar, Hong Kong, The Philippines, Malaysia, Saudi Arabia, United Kingdom, Turkey, London, Australia, Mexico, Italy, Russia, Tokyo, South Africa, Switzerland, Hungary, China, Monte Carlo, and Spain. He also owns Nobu Hotel Las Vegas and Nobu Hotel City of Dreams Manilla. Matsuhisa authored the books Nobu West, Nobu: The Cookbook, Nobu Now, and Nobu Miami: The Party Cookbook. It has been reported that the Nobu empire generates several hundred million dollars in revenue every year.
