Marc Anthony, Shannon De Lima Make Red Carpet Debut As Married Couple

June 21, 2024
blog

Love is in the air!

Marc Anthony and Shannon De Lima exemplified sheer bliss as they graced their first red carpet together since getting hitched at the Latin Grammy Awards in Las Vegas on Thursday, Nov. 20.

Venezuelan-born Lima was breathtaking in royal blue, as she showed off her supermodel shape in a long silk gown with ample sparkle embellishments. The singer’s new wife, 26, carried a silver box clutch and wore neutral makeup.

For his part, Anthony kept things dapper in a black tuxedo jacket with satin lapels, which he casually dressed down with a white shirt and dark denim jeans.

The “Vivir Mi Vida” singer, 46, hit the stage to perform a Spanish and English rendition of “Rude” with Magic! and picked up the Best Salsa Album award for 3.0.

Us Weekly confirmed the couple tied the knot on Tuesday, Nov. 11, at Anthony’s home in La Romana, Dominican Republic. Of the 100 guests invited for the nuptials were Latin music stars Juan Luis Guerra, Carlos Vives, and Tito El Bambino.

This marks Anthony’s third trip down the aisle after ending marriages with Jennifer Lopez (2014) and Dayanara Torres (2004).
