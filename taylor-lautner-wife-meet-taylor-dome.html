Taylor Lautner career

Following small roles in comedies like The Bernie Mac Show (2003) and My Wife and Kids (2004), Lautner went on to voice characters in shows like What’s New, Scooby-Doo? (2005) and Danny Phantom (2005).

Lautner played the son of the title character on the BBC sitcom Cuckoo from 2014 to 2018. He starred as Dr. Cassidy Cascade in FOX’s black comedy series Scream Queens’ second season in 2016.

Lautner’s transformation into a teen idol and sex icon in the late 2000s came about as a result of him receiving media attention for his appearance and significantly altering his physique to continue playing Jacob Black in subsequent Twilight films.

He came in at number two on Glamour’s list of “The 50 Sexiest Men of 2010” and number four on People’s list of “Most Amazing Bodies” in 2010. Lautner was crowned the highest-paid teen actor in Hollywood that same year, as well.
