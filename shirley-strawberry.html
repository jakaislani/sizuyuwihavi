Shirley Strawberry- Biography

Shirley Strawberry- Professional Career

Shirley began her career as a radio host in the mid-1980s when she joined WCGI-FM as a co-host of the show “The Doug Banks Show.” She went on to serve as a host at KKBT-FM a few years later. Her career took a sharp turn in the year 2000 when she began co-hosting the Morning Show with Steve Harvey. She worked there until the last episode in 2005, and it helped her get a lot of popularity as well as becoming the number one morning program in Los Angeles.

The Steve Harvey Morning Show was resurrected in 2005, and she returned as a co-host. She continued to work on the show and grew in popularity. She used to present a feature on the show called the Strawberry Letter Podcast, in which she discussed real-life concerns affecting women. According to her section, she also wrote a book called The Strawberry Letter: Real truth, real advise, since bitterness isn’t sexy.

The same year, she featured as a guest on one of the episodes of the American talk show “The Monique Show.” She has one acting credit to her resume as of now, from the 2009 film “Madea Goes to Jail.” She has consistently worked as a co-host with Steve Harvey on all of his Morning show initiatives, and she is also his current co-host.
