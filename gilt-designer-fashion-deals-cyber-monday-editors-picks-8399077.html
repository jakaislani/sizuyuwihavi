Elie Tahari The Jane Dress

It was love at first sight when I came across the Elie Tahari The Jane Dress. It has all my favorite components of a good fall and winter dress, and it can easily be dressed up when paired with the right shoes and layerables. The 100-percent cotton dress comes in black in sizes XS through XL, but it’s recommended that you size down, as this dress runs large. There’s a button front closure for easy on-and-off, a mandarin collar, two side pockets, long blouson sleeves with elastic at the cuff, and a belted waist. It has an A-line shape and falls slightly above the knee. 

Manolo Blahnik Hangisi Suede Flat

I am a die-hard Sex And The City fan, and while I was watching this show in awe way before it was probably appropriate for my age, I am proud to say that much of my career and fashion sense can be credited to Carrie’s style. So it should come as no surprise that a stunning pair of Manolo Blahnik Hangisi Suede Flats are on this list.

The low-to-ground style comes in a deep red shade in sizes 36 through 40. They feature a buttery soft velvet exterior with a silver-toned buckle embellished with crystals. It has a lightly padded insole for added comfort and a ¼-inch heel for a bit of lift. It's noted that this style runs small, so it’s recommended you buy a half-size up.

Joe’s Jeans Mid-Rise Clover Boot-Cut Jean

If you are in the market for a quality pair of denim, a great place to start is Joe’s Jeans. They have a great fit, come in a ton of timeless silhouettes, and they get softer and softer with every wash. This is why I plan to add the Joe’s Jeans Mid-Rise Clover Boot-Cut Jean to my collection. They come in black in sizes 24 through 33. They feature a mid-rise design and a close fit at the thigh and open out to a bootcut at the ankle. It features five functional front and back pockets and a classic button and zipper fly closure. But hurry, sizes are selling out fast.
