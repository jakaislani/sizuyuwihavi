Rashmika Mundanna is an Indian model and actress who has been working in the Kannada, Tamil and Telugu cinema and has acted in various movies. She was born on 5th April 1996, hailing from Virajpet, Karnataka, India where she grew up along with siblings and parents. She studied at MS Ramaiah College of Arts, Science, and Commerce from where she got a bachelors degree in the field of Psychology, Journalism, and English Literature. Apart from acting and modeling, she loves traveling and gymming as she is a fitness freak so she loves to work out daily. She is mainly known for the role played in the Kannada movie “Kirik Party”.

Career

In 2016, Rashmika Mundanna took the start of her professional career and started appearing in Kannada cinema where she made a debut movie “Kirik Party” for which she gained huge acclaim. Since then, she didn’t look back and moved on in her career life. Afterward, she worked in two super hit movies including “Anjani Putra” and “Chamak” for which she got much appreciation from fans and critics. She apparently appeared in a large number of movies and also marked her identity in the Tamil and Telugu cinema where she appeared in many movies. In 2018, she made a Telugu debut movie “Chalo” and fortunately, it became hit and she received much praise and appreciation from her fans and avid movie lovers. While in the same year, she worked in another rom-com movie “Geetha Govindham” which became an instant hit and rocked at the Telugu box office. In 2020, she made her Tamil debut movie “Sultan” for which she got much recognition as she gave a brilliant performance in the movie. Although, she is an emerging actress and will take time to hold a special place in the cinematic world. She is struggling to get more success in the future. In 2014, she became a brand ambassador of the “Clean & Clear Fresh Face Content” that fortunately she won. Since then, she did various modeling assignments for which she gained much recognition. One of her interviews, Ramshika Mundanna revealed that she had an inclination towards acting since her childhood and she decided to be a famous actress.
